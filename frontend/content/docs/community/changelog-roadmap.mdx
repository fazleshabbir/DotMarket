## Phase 2: Engagement & Optimization (Current)

We are currently operating in Phase 2. While the core prediction mechanics are solid, the platform requires extensive features to drive user engagement, retain liquidity, and ensure flawless performance under heavy load.

The engineering team is actively deploying updates on a weekly basis to the Arc Testnet. 

### Current Development Priorities

- **Gas Optimization:** Rewriting specific state variables within the Solidity contracts to drastically reduce the gas required to execute a prediction. Even on a cheap network, efficiency is key for high-frequency actors.
- **Dynamic Payout Display:** Overhauling the frontend logic to calculate and display the real-time payout multipliers much faster as the UP and DOWN pools fluctuate before the lock period.
- **Global Leaderboard:** Implementing an on-chain tracking system that ranks users based on their prediction win rate, total volume predicted, and longest winning streaks.
- **Automated Claiming:** Researching meta-transaction infrastructure that would allow the protocol to auto-distribute winnings back to wallets, removing the need for users to manually click "Claim" after every successful round.

<Callout type="warning" title="Testnet Volatility">
  During Phase 2, the Arc Testnet contracts may be periodically paused or upgraded without prior warning. This is a necessary step to ensure we can hot-fix bugs and implement gas optimizations rapidly.
</Callout>

## Phase 3: Mainnet & Ecosystem (Future Vision)

Phase 3 represents the culmination of our testing. Once the protocol has operated flawlessly on the Arc Testnet with thousands of concurrent users, we will prepare for real economic value.

This phase is not just about launching; it is about establishing DotMarket as the premier prediction platform in decentralized finance.

### Upcoming Milestones

#### 1. Security Audits & Mainnet Launch
Before transitioning from the Arc Testnet, the entire codebase will be frozen and submitted to multiple tier-1 blockchain security firms. Only upon receiving pristine audit reports will DotMarket be deployed to a high-throughput Mainnet ecosystem. 

#### 2. Multi-Asset Expansion
While Bitcoin (BTC) is the perfect asset for proving our 1-minute model due to its massive liquidity and Oracle reliability, our community demands more. Following mainnet stabilization, we will implement governance votes to introduce new prediction pools for assets like Ethereum (ETH) and Solana (SOL).

#### 3. Algorithmic Trading API
To cater to our most sophisticated users, we will release a comprehensive SDK and WebSocket API. This will allow developers to run algorithmic bots that ingest Oracle data and automatically execute UP or DOWN predictions based on quantitative strategies.

#### 4. Decentralized Governance (DAO)
The ultimate goal of DotMarket is absolute decentralization. The core team will slowly step back, handing over parameter control, fee management, and asset curation directly to the community via an on-chain DAO structure.
