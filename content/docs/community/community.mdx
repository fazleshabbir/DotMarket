DotMarket is more than just a smart contract protocol; it is a thriving, fast-paced ecosystem of traders, developers, and blockchain enthusiasts. Because our 1-minute prediction markets are entirely decentralized and non-custodial, the community serves as the ultimate backbone of the platform.

Whether you are looking to discuss high-frequency market strategies, contribute code to our open-source repositories, or simply hang out while watching the Bitcoin tape, there is a place for you in the DotMarket community.

<SectionDivider />

## Discord: The Trading Floor

Our Discord server is the central hub for all real-time communication. Given that DotMarket resolves rounds every 60 seconds, the pace of conversation in our channels is electric. We have structured our Discord to resemble a modern trading floor, prioritizing speed, alpha, and camaraderie.

### Key Channels

- **#announcements**: The only place for official updates from the core team regarding protocol upgrades, testnet incentives, and scheduled downtime.
- **#the-pit**: The main general chat. Discuss live Bitcoin price action, share your prediction streaks, and react to volatile market swings in real-time.
- **#strategy-and-alpha**: A dedicated channel for data-driven predictors. Discuss order flow, volume spikes, and macro events that might influence the next 1-minute candle.
- **#testnet-feedback**: Actively playing on the Arc Testnet? Drop your UI/UX suggestions, feature requests, and general feedback here. The core team reads every single message.
- **#support-tickets**: If you run into a critical bug or need assistance with your wallet connection, open a ticket here to communicate directly with our moderation team.

<Callout type="info" title="Weekly Community Calls">
  Every Friday at 15:00 UTC, the core development team hosts a community stage in Discord to discuss the past week's volume metrics, review upcoming features, and answer questions directly from the audience.
</Callout>

DotMarket is committed to the ethos of Web3. While we are currently in an intensive beta phase on the Arc Testnet, our long-term vision requires total transparency. 

Our GitHub organization will soon house the entire infrastructure of the protocol:
- **Smart Contracts:** The core Solidity architecture powering the 1-minute markets, the pari-mutuel payout calculations, and the Oracle interactions.

## The Path to Decentralized Governance

Currently, the DotMarket core team manages the protocol parameters to ensure agility during the Arc Testnet phase. However, a decentralized prediction market must ultimately be governed by its active participants.

In the future, DotMarket aims to transition towards a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) model. The active community will have direct influence over the protocol's trajectory.

### Potential Future Governance Roles

- **Asset Curation:** Voting on which assets (e.g., ETH, SOL) should be added alongside Bitcoin for new 1-minute prediction pools.
- **Fee Structures:** Adjusting the protocol fee percentage and deciding how the treasury funds are allocated (e.g., developer grants, liquidity incentives, or token buybacks).
- **Oracle Adjustments:** Voting on incorporating fallback oracles or changing data providers to ensure maximum uptime and price accuracy.

Participating heavily in the testnet phase, providing actionable feedback, and engaging constructively in Discord are the best ways to establish yourself as a community leader ahead of our eventual transition to community governance.

## Getting Support

We understand that interacting with smart contracts, even on a testnet, can sometimes be confusing. If you encounter issues, follow these steps to get help rapidly:

1. **Consult the FAQ:** Before reaching out, review the extensive FAQ in this documentation. 90% of common questions regarding payouts, ties, and 1-minute mechanics are answered there.
2. **Ask in Discord:** The `#support-tickets` channel is your best resource. Our global moderation team is highly responsive. 
3. **Provide Details:** When asking for help, always provide your public wallet address and the specific transaction hash on the Arc Testnet block explorer. Never share your private keys or seed phrase.

<Callout type="error" title="Security Warning">
  Official DotMarket moderators and team members will **NEVER** direct message (DM) you first. We will never ask you to click an external link to "validate your wallet" or "sync your account." If someone claiming to be support DMs you, block and report them immediately.
</Callout>
